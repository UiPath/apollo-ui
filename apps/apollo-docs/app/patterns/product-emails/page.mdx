# Product emails

Product emails — including transactional emails and notifications — are structured communications that combine content, layout, and design elements to deliver a specific message or action to users outside the main application UI.

## Overview

Product emails incorporate standard components (buttons, headers, images) and follow foundational design rules, but are tailored to email-specific contexts. They represent a design pattern for communication flows that occur outside the product interface.

## Email types

Product emails are used across UiPath products and cover a range of scenarios:

- **Automation Cloud notifications** — platform-level updates and alerts
- **Invoices** — billing and subscription communications
- **Orchestrator notifications** — job status and error summaries
- **Task Mining updates** — analysis completion and results
- **Verify email** — account verification during signup
- **Reset password** — account recovery
- **Orchestrator error summary** — aggregated error reports
